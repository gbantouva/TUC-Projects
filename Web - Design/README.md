# Sexally - A No-Shame Sex Education Platform

**Course**: Web Design  
Technical University of Crete - School of Electrical and Computer Engineering  
**Team Members**: Alexandra Gkaragkani, Georgia Bantouva, Olga Lantzouraki, Eva Pantazi

## Overview

A comprehensive online platform providing pleasure-focused sex education for teens and adults. The website aims to break taboos and foster open dialogue around sexuality through educational resources, interactive tools, and community engagement.

## Key Features

**Interactive Main Page**
- Central hub with clear navigation
- Catchy headlines and call-to-action buttons
- Introduction to platform mission and offerings

**Expert Articles**
- Informative content on sexual health, pleasure, and relationships
- Topics include consent, STIs, periods, and sexual safety
- Authored by field experts

**Engaging Quizzes**
- Interactive learning tools
- Topics covering sexual health, consent, and pleasure
- Knowledge testing and reinforcement

**Community Chatrooms**
- Safe and inclusive discussion spaces
- Peer-to-peer and expert interactions
- Support and meaningful conversations about sexuality

**Contact Resources**
- Dedicated contact information page
- Clear communication channels for inquiries and support

## Technologies

- **HTML5**: Content organization and structure
- **CSS3**: Vibrant black and pink theme, responsive layouts
- **JavaScript**: Interactive features, form validation, quiz functionality, menu toggles

## Design Philosophy

The platform combines informative content with engaging features and user-friendly design to create a valuable resource for comprehensive, pleasure-focused sex education. The codebase is well-organized for easy maintenance and future expansion.

## Website Structure

- Main landing page with platform introduction
- Articles section with expert content
- Interactive quizzes for knowledge testing
- Community chatrooms for discussions
- Contact page for user support
